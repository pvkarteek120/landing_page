Courses We Offer

In Bachelor Of Technology

CSE

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) branch focuses on the study of computer systems, software development, and computational theories. It covers programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and computer architecture, preparing students for careers in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more. CSE graduates are equipped to solve complex problems using technology, making them valuable in various industries.

ECE

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) branch combines the study of electronic systems, communication technologies, and signal processing. It covers areas like circuit design, embedded systems, telecommunications, and VLSI design, preparing students for roles in industries such as telecommunications, consumer electronics, and robotics. ECE graduates have versatile skills that allow them to innovate and optimize electronic and communication systems in various sectors.

EEE

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) branch focuses on the study of electrical systems, electronics, and power generation. It covers areas such as circuit analysis, control systems, power systems, and renewable energy technologies, preparing students for careers in power generation, automation, and electrical design. EEE graduates are equipped to work on sustainable energy solutions, electrical infrastructure, and cutting-edge electronic devices across diverse industries.

CIVIL

The Civil Engineering branch in B.Tech focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. It involves subjects like structural engineering, geotechnics, and environmental engineering. Civil engineers play a critical role in developing sustainable, safe, and efficient infrastructure, combining technical skills with project management.

About Us

Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College was established in the year 1973 by the “ Society for Collegiate Education “ located in the heart of Visakhapatnam city in a sprawling 10 acre campus by socially conscious eminent and philanthropic persons from different walks of life like Education, Indian Administrative Service and Business with a service motive and named the college in honour of Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya,who was the then Vice Chancellor of Andhra University.The college is affiliated to Andhra University and accredited by NAAC with ‘ A’ grade.MBA, MCA and Engineering courses are approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college is offering a wide range of programs from Intermediate (plus 2 level )to Post Graduation and Research
The college has excellent infrastructure and state of the art equipment in Laboratories. Other facilities like Seminar halls, Libraries, Playfields, Bank / ATM etc are also available. The college emphasizes on and encourages Sports , NCC, NSS to a great extent. We have a large pool of talented sports men / women who brought laurels to the college. Talent in Sports students is recognized and offered scholarships. The college offers on the campus NCC training in all the three wings. Army, Navy and Air Force. We also have an NSS unit on the campus that inculcate in students the sense of service. The Skill Development Center is engaged in training the students in social and employable skills, that give a competitive edge to our students and are necessary for Placements and other competitive exams. The college offer placement services to our students. Every year more than 20 companies visit the campus for placement drives and nearly 250 of our out - going eligible students secure employment.
